# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We as contributors and maintainers pledge to make participation in this project a harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:

- Being respectful and constructive
- Giving and accepting feedback gracefully
- Focusing on project goals and shared quality standards

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- Harassment, insults, or personal attacks
- Publishing private information without permission
- Disruptive or bad-faith interactions

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing standards.

If you encounter unacceptable behavior, please report it through the security/contact channel described in [`SECURITY.md`](./SECURITY.md).
