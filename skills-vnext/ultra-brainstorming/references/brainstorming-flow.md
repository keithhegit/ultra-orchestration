# Brainstorming Flow

This skill borrows heavily from the strongest `superpowers` idea: design before
implementation, even for work that feels simple.

## Sequence

1. explore context
2. ask one question at a time
3. present 2-3 approaches
4. recommend one
5. present the design
6. write the design artifact
7. self-review for ambiguity and scope creep
8. hand off to planning

## Anti-Pattern

Do not say "this is too small to need a design." Small tasks still benefit from
clear success criteria and non-goals.
